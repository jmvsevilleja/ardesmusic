MUSIC LESSONS, COACHING, TUTORIALS & WORKSHOPS AVAILABLE

FLUTE & CLARINET

Flute & Clarinet are wind instruments offered as individual lessons. If you are a student who desires to join the band in school, be knowledgeable of the requirements, and perform the instrument with confidence. Our teachers can help you!

Students are from beginners to experienced or advanced—ages ranging from 7 years old to adults.

PIANO & KEYBOARD

The piano is a large keyboard musical instrument with a wooden case enclosing a soundboard and metal strings, which are struck by hammers when the keys are depressed. School offers a variety of studies: from Baroque, Classical, Romantic to Modern alternatives.

Students are from beginners to experienced or advanced—ages ranging from 4 years old to adults. Music Theory, Sight-Reading, and Technique integrated on the individual session of each student.

Click here to join the best line-up of students and teachers of our school.

Guitar

The guitar is a stringed musical instrument, with a fretted fingerboard, typically incurved sides, and six or twelve strings, played by plucking or strumming with the fingers or a plectrum.

Private instruction in popular styles: rock, blues, jazz, classical, fingerstyle, folk is available on acoustic, electric guitar, and bass guitar. Teachers are experienced in classical solo and band performing teaching.

Students of all ages, from beginners, levels of experience through advanced-ages ranging from 7 years old to adults, are accepted.

Click here to join our line-up of students in our school.

VIOLIN,VIOLA,CELLO

A violin is a "little viola," a violone is a "big viola" or a bass violin, and a violoncello (often abbreviated cello) is a "small violone" (or literally, a "small big viola"). These are all instrumental stringed instruments played with a horsehair bow. It has four strings and a body of characteristic rounded shape, narrowed at the middle and with two f-shaped sound holes.

Beginning lessons in a Violin, Viola, and Cello offered from ages six and above. Rental of instruments are offered through recommended stores by our teachers. Contact us now and received the opportunity to learn this instrument.

Click here to join our line-up of students in our school.

VOICE & PERSONALITY DEVELOPMENT

Voice student and coaching include classical to popular styles, from blues, jazz, pop, and folk. Our teachers teach the student to find their sound. Teaching vocal tools in the right way a student possesses to get the best possible results are the essentials of maximizing the potential of a student to be a singer.

An elective course (Kids & Teens Personality Development Class) integrated and offered to every student registered at the school.

Students from beginners, with levels of experience through advanced-ages ranging from 9 years old to adults, are accepted.

Click here to join our line-up of students in our school.

DRUMS & PERCUSSION

Drums and Percussion Instrument sounded by striking with sticks or the hands, typically cylindrical, barrel-shaped, or bowl-shaped, with a taut membrane over one or both ends.

Students will learn how to play on a full drum set, as well as improve their rhythmic accuracy and accentuation on a single snare drum. Students taught how to read and perform a variety of notes and rhythms. Additional percussion instruments introduced to strengthen their musical foundation further.

Students from beginners, with levels of experience through advanced-ages ranging from 4 years old to adults, are accepted.

WHO WE ARE

The Arts Development School of Music (Ar-De-School of Music) in Bellflower, California, is a private learning center in the field of arts and music established on November 23, 1994. Its purpose is to integrate every individual's educational growth and development through learning and enjoyment of arts and music that promotes student's maturity in their creative thinking and expression.

Ar-De-School of Music offers music lessons in the communities and cities of Bellflower, Artesia, Norwalk, Lakewood, Downey, Paramount, Compton, Lynwood, Cerritos, Cypress, La Palma, Santa Fe Springs, Bixby Knolls, La Mirada, Whittier, Buena Park, Los Alamitos, Pico Rivera, Hawaiian Gardens, Bell Gardens, Bell, South Gate, Huntington Park, Commerce, Montebello, Anaheim, Fullerton, Signal Hill, Long Beach, Carson, Torrance, Gardena, Lawndale and more.

CERTIFICATES, ACCREDITATION MEMBERSHIPS & LICENSES

The Ar-De-School of Music teachers are certified graduates of recognized Music & Art Universities/Institutions worldwide.

Primary faculty teachers are members of the MTAC-Music Teachers Associations of California, NGPT-National Guild of Piano Teachers, ACM-American College of Musicians, MTNA-Music Teachers National Association, CAPMT-California Association of Professional Music Teachers, ABRSM (Associated Board of the Royal Schools of Music).

The teaching Assistants are Ar-De-School of Music certified and have a resume of teaching experience. Student Assistants are merit recipients in their level of attainment.

The Arts Development School of Music is licensed to operate under the ordinances of the city of Bellflower in California.
